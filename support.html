Support — DotChroma

DotChroma is a color picker for iPhone and iPad: sample colors from photos, explore dominant palettes, browse history, export HEX/RGB/HSL codes, and check WCAG contrast — all processed locally on your device.

Contact

Email: sramezani52@gmail.com

We reply in French or English. When reporting a bug, include your iOS version and the app version below.

Quick help

  • Import an image or use the camera, then tap the image to sample a color.
  • Use the History, Codes, or Contrast tabs as needed.
  • Camera and save-to-photo-library are optional in Settings.

App version: 1.0 (build 1)
